Mozilla Open Design



Being part of mozilla the open design team aims to bring open source principles to the design world in an unprecedented way.
All ideas are generated by the community are considered open source and made available through Creative Commons.

The team's website https://blog.mozilla.org/opendesign/

Today the Mozilla community is made up of a thousand-plus staff members and more than 10,000 volunteer contributors.
Following the ideals of open source work the team follows a call and response approach to design.
The team's current focus is renewing Mozilla's brand identity

Localization Team

The team responsible for translating mozilla products.
Translation takes more than just knowing the language, a translator must be familiar with the culture for it to work hence "localization".

Most Mozillians speak at least 2 languages.

Today most mozilla products and websites are available in 74 languages
